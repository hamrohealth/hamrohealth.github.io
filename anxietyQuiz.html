Anxiety Self-Assessment

This self-assessment has questions that will help figure out whether you are showing any warning signs of anxiety. Please read each question carefully, and answer how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past few weeks. This test will not give you a diagnosis, but it will give you a better idea of how you are feeling and what the next steps could be.


How often do you feel nervous or anxious and find it difficult to control?






Does worry or anxiety make you feel fatigued or irritable?






Does worry or anxiety affect your sleep or ability to concentrate?






How often do you feel afraid as if something awful might happen?






Experience strong fear that causes panic, shortness of breath, chest pains, a pounding heart, sweating, shaking, nausea, dizziness, and/or fear of dying?






Do you ever engage in repetitive behaviors to manage your worry? (checking if the oven is off, locking doors, washing hands, counting, repeating words)






Do you ever avoid places or social situations for fear of this panic?
