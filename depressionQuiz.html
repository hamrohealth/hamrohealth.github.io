Depression Self-Assessment

This self-assessment has questions that will help figure out whether you are showing any warning signs of depression. Please read each question carefully, and answer how often you have experienced the same or similar challenges in the past few weeks. This test will not give you a diagnosis, but it will give you a better idea of how you are feeling and what the next steps could be.


Little interest or pleasure in doing things






Feeling down, depressed, irritable, or hopeless






Trouble falling asleep, staying asleep, or sleeping too much






Feeling tired or having little energy






Poor appetite or overeating






Feeling bad about yourself—or feeling that you have let yourself down






Trouble concentrating on things, such as school, work, reading, or watching television






Moving or speaking so slowly that other people could have noticed. Or the opposite - being so fidgety or restless that you were moving around a lot






Suicidal thoughts, or thoughts of hurting yourself






If you have had any days with the issues above, how difficult have these problems made it for you at work, home, school, or with other people?
