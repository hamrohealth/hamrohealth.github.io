Understanding Intellectual Disabilities

  • Begins before the age of 18
  • Usually genetic
  • Affects capability to learn/problem solve
  • Affects skills practiced in everyday life
  • Can be mild, moderate, or severe

Affected Social Skills

  • Communication
  • Social Cues
  • Social Responsiblities

Affected Conceptual Skills

  • Language/Literacy
  • Time
  • Money
  • Direction

Affected Practical Skills

  • Self-Efficacy within occupation?
  • Personal Care
  • Transportation
  • Resources

How to assist those with intellectual disabilities

  • Patience (individuals with intellectual disabilities have the capability to learn, they may just need extra time and guidance, or adaptations).
  • Encourage independence and personal responsibility.
